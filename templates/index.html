Understanding Financial News Sentiment

Financial sentiment analysis is a technique used to analyze news, reports, and market data to understand investor sentiment. By processing news headlines and articles, machine learning models can determine whether the market is moving in a positive, negative, or neutral direction. This helps investors and analysts make better decisions based on real-time insights.
